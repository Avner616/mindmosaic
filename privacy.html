Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

MindMosaic ("we," "us," "the site") provides free personality, strengths, and intelligence tests for entertainment and self-reflection. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use the site.

Your test answers

Every test on MindMosaic is scored entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your answers are never sent to, or stored on, any server we operate. If you close the tab or refresh the page, your answers and results are gone. We do not create accounts, profiles, or any record tied to you personally from your test activity.

Advertising & cookies

MindMosaic is free to use and is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to show ads, measure performance, and, where permitted, personalize ads based on your browsing activity across sites.

We don't control what data Google's advertising system collects or how it's used beyond displaying ads on this site. You can learn more about Google's practices and manage your ad personalization settings here:

  • How Google uses information from sites that use its services
  • Google Ads Settings — opt out of personalized ads
  • Digital Advertising Alliance — additional opt-out tools
If you're in the EU/UK, you may see a separate cookie consent prompt from Google's Consent Management Platform the first time you visit — this lets you accept or limit ad cookies before any are set.

What we don't do

  • We don't require sign-up, email addresses, or accounts to use any test.
  • We don't sell personal data — we have none to sell.
  • We don't use your test answers for anything other than showing you your own results, live, on your own screen.

Analytics

We may use standard, privacy-respecting analytics (such as aggregate visit counts) to understand which tests are popular and improve the site. This data is anonymized and not linked to individual test answers.

Children's privacy

MindMosaic is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
