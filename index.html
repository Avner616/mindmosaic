Questions people ask

Is it really free?

Yes — every test, every result, forever. The site is supported by clearly-labelled sponsored placements instead of paywalls.

Are these clinical diagnoses?

No. These tests are for self-reflection and entertainment. They are inspired by popular frameworks (Myers-Briggs–style types, HIGH5-style strengths, Big Five research, Enneagram tradition) but they are not medical, psychological, or educational assessments. If something here raises a real concern, talk to a qualified professional.

Do you store my answers?

No. All scoring happens in your browser. Refresh the page and it's gone.

Why do I see an ad before my results?

One short sponsored message after each test is what keeps the site free. Close it and your results appear immediately — no tricks, no email walls.

How accurate is the IQ test?

It's a 15-item reasoning challenge — a fun estimate, not a proctored psychometric exam. Treat the score as a warm-up for your brain, not a verdict on it.
